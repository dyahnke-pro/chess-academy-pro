# Launch Playbook — Chess Academy Pro

Marketing materials for getting the first 100 users. Pure copy and
process — no infrastructure, no signups required to use any of
this. Updated as new templates are added.

## 1. 60-second demo video — script

**Format:** screen recording with voiceover. Goal: someone in the
chess subreddit watches this and clicks the link. **Don't tell, show.**

**Pre-record checklist:**
- App is on the preview URL (or production once live)
- Browser zoom at 110% so chat text is readable
- Mic on quietly (or no mic — text overlays work fine for r/chess)
- Cursor highlighter on if your screen recorder supports it
- Imported games already populated (don't waste 10s showing import)
- Practice the script once — natural beats polished

**Script — 60 seconds, six beats:**

> **[0:00–0:06] HOOK — show coach window already open with a real
> question pre-typed.** Voiceover or caption:
>
> *"I built a chess coach that actually watches your games."*

> **[0:06–0:18] PROOF — type "what's my worst opening?" and let it
> stream the answer.** The coach says something specific from the
> imported games, like *"You've played the Sicilian 9 times — only
> 22% win rate. You keep allowing the Grand Prix Attack."*
>
> Caption: *"It pulls from games you've actually played."*

> **[0:18–0:30] AGENT MOMENT — type "play the King's Indian Attack
> against me" and let it auto-start a game.** Show the engine
> playing 1.Nf3, 2.g3, 3.Bg2 — actually KIA, not generic Stockfish.
>
> Caption: *"Tell it what to do — it does the thing."*

> **[0:30–0:42] NARRATION — make a move, hear the coach speak.**
> Coach narrates: *"You played e4 — that's the Italian Game
> opening. I'll play Nf6 to challenge your center."*
>
> Caption: *"Voice-coached. Pause, ask questions, interrupt."*

> **[0:42–0:54] INSIGHT CARD — open Game Insights, show the
> "Beat a 2100" or "Win 72% with the Italian" card, tap Share.**
>
> Caption: *"Stats worth sharing. Your chess, in a nutshell."*

> **[0:54–0:60] CTA — landing page with pricing.**
>
> Caption: *"Free during beta. Lock in $2.99/month for life.
> chessacademy.pro"*

**Production tips:**
- Record at 1920x1080. Crop to vertical 9:16 for X / Reels later.
- Background music: skip. Voiceover or silence is more trustworthy
  for a solo-built tool.
- End frame should be the URL, held for 2 seconds, big text.
- First upload: Twitter/X with caption "I built this. Free during
  beta. [link]". Pin to profile.
- Recut for: r/chess (longer description, video link), Product
  Hunt (square crop), Instagram Reels (vertical).

**Length variants:**
- 30s: cut beats 2 + 4 (proof + insight). Use for X.
- 90s: extend beat 3 (agent moment) — show 2-3 different intent
  phrases. Use for YouTube / Reddit.
